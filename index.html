Our project is a remarking of Crash Bandicoot, which is a 2D version game of it.

Central

Crash Bandicoot is 2D pixel game experience. It’s a rehash of Naughty Dog’s classic game Crash Bandicoot. Taking inspiration from the graphics and background of retro games, it drops the player into an epic story. With more background than a usual rhythm game, Crash Bandicoot has a complex scenario and rich interactivity, which will immerse you in the game.

In the game, the player needs to control an additive raccoon in various scenarios for adventure. Are you interested in it??? Download it and join us soon!!

Scenario

Crash is a adventurous animal. On a journey, he met a doctor Aku Aku. When he learns that Crash is out to adventure, Aku Aku joins him on his quest. Being a spiritual entity, Aku Aku protects and communicates with Crash through the aid of a magical, floating mask. What adventures would they have on their journey?
